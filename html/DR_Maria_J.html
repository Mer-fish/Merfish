Dr. Maria J
Qualification: BASLP, MASLP, M.A (Ling), Ph.D (Linguistics (Interdisciplinary))

CRR No :A34685
About
Dr. Maria J, presently an Associate Professor in the department of Speech – Language Pathology and the Academic Co-ordinator for the B.ASLP course conducted at MERF-ISH has an experience of over 9 years in the field. Her major area of interest lies in the field of Discourse Analysis in various Communication Disorders, exploring innovative teaching methods and developing Indianized Assessment materials, Intervention manuals and Tele-Therapy Materials.
PG Dissertation: An Intervention Programme for Improving Phonological Sensitivity in Children – Dissertation submitted to The TN DR MGR Medical University – June 2013
PhD Thesis ‘Discourse Analysis in Individuals with Neuro-communication Disorders – A Compilation Study’ submitted at Department of CAS Linguistics; Annamalai University – Awarded Ph.D degree in January 2023.
