About
Mr. Pachaiappan, with an experience of 5+ years in the field post MASLP has been involved in conducting Camps at various places in Tamil Nadu which is organized by MERF-ISH. He has been specialized in Electrophysiological tests, Implant audiology, Telehealth audiology, Hearing Conservation Program, New born screening, Community out-reach programs, Hearing aid fitting and programming, Preoperative and post-operative counselling
