About
Ms. Asvitha .R ,presently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Audiology at MERF-ISH and Clinical Incharge at MERF Hospital has an experience of 2+ years in the field specialized Diagnostic test procedures in Audiology ,Implantable Hearing Devices and Management of Tinnitus . She is specialized in Electrically evoked auditory brainstem response (EABR), Intra –operative measures, Maping ,Troubleshooting in Cochlear Implants and also pre-operative counselling .
