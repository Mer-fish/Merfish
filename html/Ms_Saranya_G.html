Ms.Saranyaa G
Qualification - MSc.Audiology

About
Ms. Saranyaa, presently an Associate Professor in the department of Audiology and the Academic Co-ordinator for the PG courses conducted at MERF-ISH has an experience of over 10 years in the field. Her major area of interest lies in Diagnostic Audiology, Vestibular Sciences and Implant Audiology.
Work Experience
Lecturer / Clinical Supervisor - 10/2012 to 10/2015 MERF Institute of Speech & Hearing (P) Ltd, Chennai Assistant Professor - 11/2015 to 10/2020 MERF Institute of Speech & Hearing (P) Ltd, Chennai Associate Professor - 11/2020 to Present MERF Institute of Speech & Hearing (P) Ltd, Chennai

  • The TN Dr MGR Medical University – MMC, CMC
  • SRM Institute of Science and Technology – Dept of ASLP
  • Underwent training in ASSR and Evoked Potentials at Intelligent Hearing Systems, University of Miami, USA in September 2003
  • Sri Ramachandra Medical University - SRFASLP
  • Bharathidasan University – Dept of Audiology, Holy Cross College
Conferences / workshops attended
