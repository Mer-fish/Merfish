About
Prof. Ranjith Rajeswaran, the Principal / Managing Director of MERF-Institute of Speech and Hearing; Chief Audiologist at Madras ENT Research Foundation and the Founder of Hearing for Life Foundation, a charitable trust working on Prevention, Restoration and Rehabilitation for persons with Communication Disorders has an experience of 25 years in the field of Audiology and Speech – Language Pathology. He is specialized in the field of Hearing Implants. He is one of the founder members of Cochlear Implant Group of India and HEARRING (Comprehensive Hearing Implant Centres) for research and development. Expert Advisory Committee Member: Rehabilitation Council of India. Executive Committee Member: Anna University. Member of Board of studies: The Tamil Nadu Dr.MGR Medical University. President: Indian Speech and Hearing Association – Tamil Nadu. Vice President: Cochlear Implant Group of India. Global Ambassador: International Smiling Crocodile.
